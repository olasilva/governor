Governor Agbu Kefas: "Moving Forward" Agenda

Transformative achievements under the visionary leadership of His Excellency, Governor Agbu Kefas

Educational Transformation

Free education policy implemented across all public primary and secondary schools. Over 250,000 students enrolled under the new policy. Teacher training programs revitalized with modern pedagogical approaches.

250k+ Students 500+ Schools +45% Enrollment

Infrastructure Expansion

Major road construction and rehabilitation across all 16 LGAs. Rural electrification projects reaching previously underserved communities. Urban renewal initiatives in Jalingo and other major towns.

350km Roads 80 Communities 15 Major Projects

Youth Empowerment & Digital Skills

Taraba Youth Empowerment Scheme (TAYES) providing digital skills training, entrepreneurship support, and job placement. Over 10,000 youths trained in digital literacy and vocational skills.

10k+ Trained 3,500 Placed 65% Success Rate

Healthcare Reform

Revitalization of primary healthcare centers across the state. Free maternal and child healthcare services. Recruitment of over 500 medical personnel to improve service delivery.

120 PHCs 500+ Staff 40% Coverage Increase

Security Stabilization

Community policing initiatives and conflict resolution mechanisms established. Farmer-herder tensions significantly reduced through dialogue and intervention programs.

70% Reduction 15 Peace Accords Community-led

Women Empowerment

Women enterprise support fund launched. Increased political representation for women in local governance structures. Skills acquisition programs for rural women across all LGAs.

15k+ Beneficiaries 30% Increase 40+ Appointments

What is TEISMS?

A revolutionary digital governance and electoral intelligence architecture for Taraba State

Political Asset Registry

Comprehensive database of 3,800+ political assets including ward coordinators, youth leaders, women mobilisers, and community influencers with performance scoring.

GIS Electoral Mapping

Geospatial visualization of influence clusters, risk zones, and resource deployment for precision mobilisation across all 16 LGAs.

Real-Time Field Reporting

Mobile-enabled intelligence uploads with GPS validation, multimedia reporting, and instant incident escalation from polling units.

Sentiment Analytics

AI-assisted monitoring of traditional and social media, misinformation detection, and governance perception tracking.

Youth & Women Mobilisation

Structured engagement networks and demographic-specific analytics for inclusive political participation.

Rapid Response System

Encrypted operational channels and real-time escalation to counter misinformation and political risks.
